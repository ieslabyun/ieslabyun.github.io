Short Bio

Currently, I am an Assistant Professor in School of Software at Yunnan University , China. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at Leiden University, The Netherlands, in 2017. Prior to Leiden, I obtained my bacholer and master degrees from Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi'an, China, in 2007 and 2011, respectively.
