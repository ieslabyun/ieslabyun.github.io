Course Introduction

English is a critically important skill in learning the advanced knowledge of computer science, because the majority of advanced computer technologies were developed in English country, like USA, or these fundamental， advanced and seminar results were mostly and originally reported in English articles. The purpose of this course is to help students to improve your English skills in terms of reading and communication, by reading English text book and giving English presentation.
Credit/Hours: 1 point/18 Hours
Course code: SOFT0049_1
Time: 1-18 week（every two weeks），3-4 Wednesday
Location: 力行楼4404

Grading Policy

Final grade=20% performance + 20% mid-term + 60% final exam
  • performance: your presence and pro-active attitude during lectures
  • mid-term: your presentation performance
  • final exam: a closed book exam, answering questions in English

注意:希望大家能够按时上课，积极参与课堂互动将能够得到额外的平时成绩。
